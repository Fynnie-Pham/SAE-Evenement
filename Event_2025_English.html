Program in 2025

Return of the Enghien-les-Bains Automobile Elegance Contest, which will take place on May 17 and 18, 2025. You will be able to admire numerous pre- and post-war classic cars competing for the famous Elegance Prize, awarded on Sunday by an amateur and professional jury based on specific criteria (car model, crew, overall presentation…).

Saturday's Program
From 2 PM to 6 PM:
Exhibition of vintage cars on the Lake Pier and the Thermal Baths Forecourt.
From 3 PM to 5 PM:
Discovery rides aboard vintage vehicles.

Departure from the Thermal Baths Forecourt – Fee: €5 (1 person), €8 (2 people), €10 (3 people).
The proceeds will benefit the charitable works of the Lions Club of Enghien-Montmorency.

Sunday's Program
From 11 AM to 12 PM:
Parade of vintage cars in the city.
From 3 PM to 5 PM:
Automobile Elegance Contest in front of the Lake Pier.
With the award ceremony at 5 PM.

Throughout the weekend, a retrospective exhibition of Enghien-les-Bains automobile elegance contests will be on display at the Kiosk in the Rose Garden.

As you can see, this "Elegance in Automobiles" event, organized by the Tourist Office, continues the tradition of automobile elegance contests held in Enghien since 1948. A visit to the spa town is highly recommended.

Saturday, May 18, 2024, from 2 PM to 6 PM, and Sunday, May 19, 2024, from 11 AM to 5 PM - Lake Pier and the Thermal Baths Forecourt, 69 Rue du Général de Gaulle, Enghien – Free entry.
